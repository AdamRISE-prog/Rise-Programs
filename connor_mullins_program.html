Goals & Focus
Primary Goals
Build a repeatable mechanical base before adding intensity
Improve joint mobility and tissue health through daily prep
Progressively develop arm speed through weighted ball and pulldown work
Build total body strength while keeping the arm healthy
Block Structure
Weeks 1–3 — Base building and velocity development
Weeks 4–5 — Pulldown introduction and volume increase
Week 6 — Planned deload for adaptation and recovery
Weeks 7–8 — Mound integration and peak output
